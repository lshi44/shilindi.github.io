Meet this Yokai, a tanuki

Tanuki, also know as Japanese raccoon dog, is a kind of mammal that lives in Asian. Mononoke in Japanese refers to the supernatural sprites. In Japanese folklore, tanuki is depicted as the master of disguising and shapeshifting.

Turing into an human being

The tanuki in your human eyes are cute and harmless animals. However, you cannot never know what our real life look like. As a tanuki live within the human beings, our family need to fake some human portraitures and change to that face and body. Disguising is not a innate talents. Our Tanukis need to study it very hard in order to be a proper mononoke, rather than a ordinary animal.
