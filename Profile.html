Early years

Hoshino Takumi is a Japanese self-called artist. He was born in 1959 in Kamakura. His father is a fisherman and his mother is a house wife. man Because Kamakura is the place where Oba Yozo tried to committed suicide in Osama Dazai's Novel (No longer Human), Takumi loved his hometown and decided to be a novelist as Dazai. However, during his school years, Takumi did terrible job in literature. In fact, he could hardly pass any of his classes. Then he fall in love with manga and started to copy the images. After finishing his high school, Takumi quit the school and decided to be a cartoonist. He is passion about manga, but has no talent in it. Takumi stayed with his parents for three years while making cartoons. Unluckily, none of his work was accepted by the publishers. Then he was kicked out by his father.

Being an Cartoonist and end up in a grocery store

Takumi took the train to Osaka, where he lived in a small flat and found a part time job in a construction site. In his early years, the cartoons are closely connected to his life. Although he didn't get any of his long-running manga published, some of his short ones and illustrations get published in newspapers, including Funny houses , Building of bodies. However, he still cannot make living at that time. During his worse time, Takumi only had one meal for 3 days. After leaving the construction site, Takumi found a numbers of part-time jobs, such as insect expert, security personnel, courier, housekeeping... Nevertheless, he never stick to one job for more than two years for he either quit the work or fired by the managers. Until his late thirties, Takumi find a job in a small grocery store. Though lazy as he is, Takumi is an frank man. He finally find a work that he can sticke to.

Current life

Takumi is still in Osaka, where he has been leaving for more than thirty years.This man has never think about travelling to anywhere, expect for visiting his hometown. He is still working in the grocery store as a cashier, for this is the only option of his income. Never get a chance to know any girls, Takumi live with himself for all his years. He adopted two dogs recently. One is a shiba inu dog and the other is a husky, which become his new muses in art making. His life after work is to shoot video of his dogs and make up short stories for them. Later on, Takumi will put then together to a short manga. That's right. Takumi still believes one day he could be an great cartoonist in the world.
